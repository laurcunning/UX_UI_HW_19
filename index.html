About Lauren

Learn more about Lauren
I'm an emerging user experience designer based out of Toronto, ON. I live in the city with my partner and our corgi puppy, Max. In my spare time I enjoy cooking, yoga, weight training, board games and good cup of coffee. Prior to pursuing UX/UI design, my background has been in social work. For the past five years I've working as a case manager to homeless youth. I've always been driven to work with people - offering empathy, connection and understanding towards others and their experiences. I also look to understand the cause and effect of human behaviours and want to be a contributing member in working towards a larger solution. I discovered UX/UI throughout the pandemic and have made the decisions to pursue it as a career. I've realsed my passion for design stems from how it aligns with my creative interests and desire for solving problems; while also allowing me to develop practical solutions and designs that provide individuals the best experience. What I enjoy most about UX/UI design is how it ties together human centred problems, visual and interactive design and research-based solutions. My approach to solving user problems is based on quantitative and qualitative research. I work to understand the user - uncovering their journey, pain points, needs and insights. I practice design thinking methods and understand the importance of the entire design process rather than just focusing on the end result. I find enjoyment in that the design process is not linear. I love how to provokes curiousity and creative thinking buy presenting me with new challenges, setbacks and requires iterations before reaching a breakthrough. My design process motivates me to close the gap between the user and their desired experience. I have found a passion for pursuing design I didn't know I had, and am eager to continue to learn as it is an always evolving and ongoing process.
